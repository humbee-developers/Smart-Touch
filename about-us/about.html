About Us

About The Founder

As founder at Smart Touch- Design based solutions, I have conceptualised and executed over 100 innovative social infrastructure designs to bridge gaps in quality education and health infrastructure in India. We undertake myriad creative and courageous approaches to deal with complex and context-specific educational and health needs of the children and youth in India through my designs. In that direction, we have recently created various mobile, compact, and resource-efficient infrastructure that are scalable, developed for overcrowded informal settlements with paucity of land in Mumbai.

Some of these designs include:

  • Magic Chair (patented multifunctional integrated learning aid)
  • Portable Container ECCE centres (Aanganwadis)
  • Women’s Mobile Gym on Wheels

Moreover, we are in the process of designing green energy projects like Shower on Wheels for homeless and street children in Mumbai. This will further create our interventions more climate resilient and sustainable ensuring that our interventions reach to the most marginalised sections of children and youth.

Having established myself earlier as an Artist & Youth Icon, I traverse smoothly between the worlds of entertainment and social innovation. I leverage networks, creativity and learnings from both arenas for personal and organisational growth. As an artist, I have played eminent roles in many bilingual films, plays and as news anchor. I have also had the privilege to be an ambassador for promoting Gujarat state tourism through various documentary films with the State Government.

When I look back, there have been two very important influences in my life that also reflect in my vocational choices. My father, who worked at All India Radio inspired me to not only pursue my artistic and creative interests but also emphasised on exploring and excelling in different aptitudes that one has. When my elder sister who leads a social development organisation inspired me to brush up my interests and skills in design, to help her in one of her community projects, I quickly agreed to take up the challenge. Later, that one decision to help my sister, became a life altering one, as today I combine my knowledge in science and technology with design to cater to the needs of the last mile and underserved populations.

My education in Management from IIM Ahmedabad and a foundation in Engineering enables me to lead financially prudent decisions at my organisation, while bringing the latest science and technology approaches to our design based community projects. In addition, being a public figure from the media, provides a unique vantage point for successfully building an influencer network and fostering effective public engagement.

Throughout this journey of an artist and an emerging social entrepreneur, what held my passion accountable was the value of resilience in thought and action. Working in both the social work and television industry is extremely challenging as there are multiple instances where your patience is tested, it is then that my resilience holds me back. This in addition to my ability and focus on forging healthy relationships with not just the people I directly work with but grassroots leaders & people across verticals in the Government has helped me scale our operations across states undertaking diverse projects in a short span of X years.

Traversing through these versatile roles with extreme focus, while being on the edge many times, has been a rewarding experience. Recently, I was felicitated with “GLORIOUS GUJARAT USA” award for contribution to the development of the society and was fortunate to be “THE CULTURAL AMBASSADOR” between two countries. Further, I was also invited for the Professional Fellowship Program by US State Dept. (2019) which played an immense role in guiding me to execute the recent social innovations. Moreover, our SMART Container Anganwadi project was selected as top 10 innovative projects by Maharashtra Government.

The leadership training and cultural immersions at HISA impact cruise will help me connect and get mentorship from stalwarts of positive change makers, giving an opportunity to learn from their decades of experience in transformational practices. It will help me build strong networks with a cohort of like-minded social entrepreneurs who share similar goals and challenges; providing an opportunity to brainstorm solutions together.
